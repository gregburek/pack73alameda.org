A Year of Fun & Adventure!

2025-2026 Year Activities

Camping & Overnights

Get ready for amazing sleepovers! This year we're planning a special campout at the Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk in the fall and another great overnight camping adventure in the spring. Get your sleeping bags ready!

Signature Events

Our year is packed with can't-miss events! We'll help our community with Scouting for Food, race in the Pinewood Derby, and celebrate our Arrow of Light scouts as they bridge over at the Blue & Gold banquet.

Outdoor Fun

Let's get outside! We'll kick off the year with an outdoor activity, go on a pack hike in the fall, enjoy a bike ride in the spring, and help keep our shores beautiful with a beach cleanup day.

Creative & Community

Show off your creative side! We'll have a tie-dye party, celebrate Halloween, make ornaments, go caroling for the holidays, and put on a show with songs and skits. We'll also celebrate Earth Day with a special activity.

Pinewood Derby

The ultimate test of creativity and speed! Design, build, and race your very own wooden car against your fellow scouts. It's one of the most exciting events of the year, with trophies and bragging rights on the line!

Character & Leadership

Builds character, leadership, and confidence through safe, hands-on fun. Scouts learn teamwork and make friends while families grow together.

Powered by Parents!

Our pack is 100% volunteer-run. That means you—our amazing parents—are the leaders who make all the fun and adventure happen. Scouting is a family activity, and it works because everyone pitches in.

Whether it's leading an activity, helping with snacks, or organizing a carpool, every little bit of help creates a huge impact for our scouts. It's a fantastic way to make memories with your child and be part of a great community. We're excited to have you on the team!
